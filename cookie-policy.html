Cookie Policy - Ixaria

Last updated: 07.04.2026

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Ixaria uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

At Ixaria, we are committed to minimizing data collection and respecting user privacy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are commonly used to:

  • Ensure website functionality
  • Analyze traffic and usage
  • Store user preferences
  • Enable marketing and advertising

3. Do We Use Cookies?

No - Ixaria does not use cookies.

We do not place, store, or access any cookies on your device when you visit our website.

This means:

  • No tracking cookies
  • No analytics cookies
  • No marketing cookies
  • No third-party cookies

4. Do We Use Similar Technologies?

We aim to avoid any technologies that track or identify users across sessions.

However, our website may still involve minimal technical processes necessary for basic functionality (such as server-side logs or security mechanisms), which:

  • Do not store data on your device
  • Do not track you across websites
  • Are not used for profiling or marketing

5. Third-Party Content

Our website may include links or references to third-party websites.

If you access those websites, they may use cookies independently. We do not control or take responsibility for their practices.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy if our practices change. Any updates will be published on this page.
